Hello! My Name Is Prince

My name is Prince, also known as Princey P. I am a canine companion of three years of age, cherished and adored by my human family. I was born in Quebec and raised in Toronto. Although I do not understand French, I am fluent in English and the Ethiopian language.

I have two younger canine siblings, Coco and Hazel, with whom I enjoy spending quality time. I am a big fan of the beach and I relish the opportunity to venture outdoors, and occasionally, I may request a brief excursion for the sole purpose of experiencing the fresh air.

During these outdoor escapades, I find great pleasure in exploring the olfactory trails left by my fellow canine counterparts. Engaging with other dogs and humans is an activity that brings me immense joy and fulfillment.

I particularly enjoy going to fenced dog parks and spending hours there. My favorite parks are the Logan Park and Greenwood Park. I am a dog that truly loves and appreciates my family. Thank you.
